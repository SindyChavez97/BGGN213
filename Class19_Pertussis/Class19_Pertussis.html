Q3. Describe what happened after the introduction of the aP vaccine? Do you have a possible explanation for the observed trend?

There was an increase in cases a few years after the change in the vaccine. Perhaps the infants that were vaccinated in 1996 with the potentially less effective aP vaccine, enter school around year 2000 (when there is a small increase in cases), and with the increase contact with other children that could potentially be infected, thus leading to an increase in cases.
